TaskMaster - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

Last Updated: September 2025

TL;DR: TaskMaster is a completely offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data or information.

1. Information We Collect

TaskMaster is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information or data from users. All your tasks, notes, and app usage remain entirely on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use, process, or analyze any user data. Your information stays completely private and local to your device.

3. Data Storage

All data created within TaskMaster (tasks, categories, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not synchronized with any external servers or cloud services.

4. Third-Party Services

TaskMaster does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app operates completely independently without any external connections.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your information is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

6. Internet Connection

TaskMaster does not require an internet connection to function. The app works entirely offline, ensuring your data never leaves your device.

7. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate lock screens and security updates.

8. Children's Privacy

TaskMaster is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there are no privacy concerns regarding children's information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If future versions of TaskMaster introduce data collection features, we will update this privacy policy accordingly. The current version is completely offline and private.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data remains under your complete control on your device.
